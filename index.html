Building Foundations for Self-Sufficiency and Personal Success through Running

The Basics

Camp Objective

Young men and women who wish to maximize their potential as runners should attend this camp. Running is an activity in which everyone can win simply by running a personal best time. In order to succeed, however, each runner should know about basic training fundamentals such as tempo running, fartlek sessions, interval training, and pool running (when injured). Young men and women should also attend this camp to gain maturity, experience a feeling of self sufficiency while away from home; and acquire friendships with other runners.
