Camp Location

The camp is held in the historic and beautiful Lincoln State Park, the boyhood home of our 16th President. The park is home to more than 10 miles of spectacular running trails and a 58 acre lake great for swimming and fishing. Runners will sleep in cobins housing 8 to 16 persons. Adult male or female supervision will be present in each cabin
